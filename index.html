Sheep Box Startup Project

Sheepbox is a campus communication platform specifically designed for Xi’an Jiaotong-Liverpool University (XJTLU). It was developed and launched in collaboration with a startup team of four, including myself, and officially went live in September 2024 for campus use.

Sheepbox actively collaborates with the university to enhance administrative efficiency and facilitate communication between students, alumni, and the institution. It reduces information and resource gaps among students while improving the utilization of university resources.

Sheepbox has reached 60% of XJTLU's student body, and its user base continues to grow. The platform is now in the process of securing Angel Round funding.

Objective

Protecting the Habitat of Migratory Birds and Marine Life from Waste Invasion.

Overview

Shanghai Chongming Dongtan serves as a critical stopover along the East Asian-Australasian Flyway for migratory birds, an important wintering site for waterfowl, and a migration corridor for rare fish like the Chinese sturgeon and other economically significant species. However, it is currently under threat from invasive marine debris.

Plastic waste poses a significant threat to biodiversity. Research shows that the proportion of seabird species and marine mammals affected by plastic waste increased from 44% in 1997 to 76% in 2022, making marine plastic pollution a major threat to ocean ecosystems.

The project aims to raise public awareness of the environmental challenges facing Chongming Dongtan and draw attention to its ecosystem and wildlife. Activities include public roadshows to engage society, forums in collaboration with nearby universities such as NYU Shanghai and Duke Kunshan University, as well as discussions involving experts from marine ecology and environmental restoration institutes.

Long-Term Goal

The project aspires to reduce pollution in Chongming Dongtan, protect its wildlife, and inspire global reflection on marine conservation. By addressing the environmental issues of Chongming Island, it aims to highlight the current state of the global marine environment and biodiversity, encouraging collective action for ocean conservation.

Expected Impact

Increase public environmental awareness in Shanghai communities by over 50% through education and outreach initiatives. Reduce waste in Chongming Dongtan by approximately 40%.

XJTLU Youth · Development Talk Series

My Role

I led my team in initiating the XJTLU Youth · Development Talk Series, designed to help current students at Xi’an Jiaotong-Liverpool University (XJTLU) gain deeper insights into future industry trends and opportunities. The series aims to guide students in identifying suitable personal development paths and provides support for postgraduate applications, job-seeking, and entrepreneurial ventures.

Event format

  • Keynote sessions featuring global successful entrepreneurs.
  • Topicalized panels led by industry leaders and pioneering entrepreneurs sharing cutting-edge insights.
  • 1-on-1 mentorship dialogues, where experienced youth mentors provide personalized advice and career guidance.

Organizing Major Campus Events

As the organizer of a large-scale Campus Integration Day event, I took the lead in coordinating and managing a team of over 50 volunteers to host an event attended by nearly 600 participants. The event aimed to help first-year students gain an in-depth understanding of the university's various academic programs while fostering connections with the educational community. I also guided students in exploring cutting-edge technologies and innovations within the university's top academic disciplines.
